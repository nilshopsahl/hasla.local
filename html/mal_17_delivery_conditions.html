DELIVERY CONDITIONS

FREE DELIVERY

Note: The shipping costs or free delivery will appear automatically in the Check Out, depending on the size of your order: Sweden: All orders above 500 NOK is delivered (with MyPack) without costs.
Denmark: All orders above 600 NOK is delivered (with MyPack) without costs.
Finland: All orders above 700 NOK is delivered (with MyPack) without costs.
Germany, England, France, Ireland, Northern Ireland, Austria, Belgium, Netherlands, Luxembourg, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania:
All orders above 1500 NOK is delivered (with UPS Standard) without costs.

FREIGHT COSTS

Note: The shipping costs or free delivery will appear automatically in the Check Out, depending on the size of your order: All purchased goods are shipped with MyPack in Sweden, Denmark and Finland, and UPS for orders outside these three countries. When your order leaves our warehouse you will receive a tracking number via e-mail in order for you to track your order on www.ups.com or MyPack in Sweden/Denmark/Finland. In Sweden, Denmark and Finland you can choose your preferred pickup location for MyPack in the Check Out at www.blafre.com. The total transport cost for a MyPack in Sweden, Denmark and Finland are 79 NOK for any size(will be shown in your chosen valuta). Standard UPS freight cost in the following countries: Germany, England, France, Ireland, Northern Ireland, Austria, Belgium, Netherlands, Luxembourg, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania: 0-2 kg:149 NOK (with UPS Standard)
2-9 kg: 249 NOK (with UPS Standard)
10+ kg: 499 NOK (with UPS Standard).
Above 1500 NOK in these countries = Free shipping.
For all countries not mentioned above, UPS freight costs will be appear automatically in the Check Out.

DELIVERY TIME

The delivery time depends on where you live. We ship all orders from Gothenburg in Sweden. MyPack in Sweden, Denmark and Finland is normally delivered within 2-5 working days from the day the order was placed, depending on your address. All purchased goods are shipped with MyPack in Sweden, Denmark and Finland, and UPS for orders outside these tree countries. All delivery times are estimated depending on the carrier. UPS freight methods is named «Standard» in EU, and «Expedited» outside. Please check directly with UPS for delivery time confirmation to your exact country and location. BLAFRE AS will not be held responsible for delays with the couriers.

NON PICKED UP GOODS

f we receive packages that have not been collected, we reserve the right to charge you for all our costs that appear when handling your order and shipment. An invoice will be sent to you. Settlement of the invoice shall be within 10 days. Many thanks for your purchase!
